Changelog: Put hidden states in Bfloat16

Previously the hidden states were in fp32, since I was using the default PyTorch mixed-precision context. This means that operations like RMSNorm and the value residual from the previous record were in fp32.

It turns out these can be bf16 without losing perf => wallclock speedup
