# Distributed the overhead of Muon


## Twitter

New NanoGPT training speed record: 3.28 Fineweb val loss in 13.1 minutes

Previous record: 15.2 minutes
Changelog: distributed the overhead of Muon

Explanation: Muon has been used as the optimizer for the record since 10/04/24. 
Muon executes a Newton-Schulz iteration after each step of training. Previously, this was performed redundantly across all 8 GPUs; now it's properly distributed.
Also note: ~2ms/step was saved by upgrading from CUDA 12.2 to 12.5.
