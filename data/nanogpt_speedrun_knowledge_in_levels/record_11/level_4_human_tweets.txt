# 1024-ctx dense causal attention → 64K-ctx FlexAttention


## Twitter

New NanoGPT training speed record: 3.28 FineWeb val loss in 5.03 minutes

Previous record: 7.2 minutes
Changelog: FlexAttention with large sequence length

This record is by @KoszarskyB

This record changes both the training and validation pipelines. Previously, we were concatenating documents and then chunking them into sequences of length 1024, which ends up splitting some documents in half.
Now with FlexAttention & large seqlen, fewer documents get split, so language modeling gets easier at both train and val time. As a result, this record gets a somewhat reduced accuracy of ~29% on HellaSwag, whereas previous records and @karpathy's original training get ~30%.
