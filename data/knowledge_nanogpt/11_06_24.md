Here's a new NanoGPT speedrun record: 3.28 FineWeb val loss in 8.2 minutes on 8xH100

Previous record: 10.8 minutes
Changelog:
- architectural shortcuts
- momentum warmup
- tanh logit capping

Shortcuts: We tweaked the transformer architecture such that later blocks can now directly access (1) the values computed in the first block and (2) the token embedding.

Each of these changes contributes a chunk of performance. Put together they add up to a 32% speedup over the prior record.

Momentum warmup: Rather than fix the Muon optimizer's momentum at 0.95 throughout training, we warm it up over the first 500 steps from 0.85 to 0.95.

Tanh logit capping: We limit the logit magnitudes to ±30 via tanh clamping, following Gemma 2.
